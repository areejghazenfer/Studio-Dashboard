How it works

Simple by design.

Three steps to a calmer, more consistent version of yourself.

01

Set your goals

Add the habits you want to build and the projects you're working toward. Break them into small, doable tasks — not a giant to-do list.

02

Check in daily

Open the app each day, tick off what you did, and watch your streak grow. Your companion reacts in real time — and one minute is all it takes.

03

Watch yourself grow

As your completion rate climbs, your companion evolves from Seed to Blooming. Look back at your stats and feel the weight of everything you've done.
